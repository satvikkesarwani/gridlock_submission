**The Problem**
Major events cause gridlock. Today, traffic teams rely on guesswork because event impact isn't quantified in advance, leading to poor resource deployment and zero post-event learning.

**Our Solution**
Gridlock Intelligence System is an AI-powered traffic command center that forecasts event delays and recommends exact staffing and barricade deployments before the jam happens.

**How It Works**
* **Predicts Delay:** Input event details; our ML model instantly forecasts the traffic delay and clearance time.
* **Optimizes Resources:** Automatically calculates the exact number of officers, volunteers, and barricades needed.
* **Live Control:** A WebSocket dashboard tracks real-time speeds and incidents during the event.
* **Post-Event Debrief:** Compares our forecast vs. actual traffic to improve future planning.

**How It's Different**
* **Proactive, not Reactive:** Standard maps tell you you're in traffic; we help prevent the traffic.
* **Operational Output:** We don't just show data—we give ground teams an exact deployment plan.
* **Closed-Loop:** Built-in analytics ensure every event makes the system smarter.
